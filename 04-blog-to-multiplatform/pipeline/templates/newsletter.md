## Newsletter Section Template

### Structural constraints
- **Body length:** 150–250 words (slight overflow acceptable if the insight demands it)
- **Subject line:** Required. 6–10 words. Specific. No clickbait punctuation.
- **Format:** Prose paragraphs. No bullet lists unless the atom set is genuinely list-structured.
- **Link prompt:** Required at the end — one clear "Read the full post →" or equivalent.

### Structural pattern
```
[SUBJECT LINE: 6–10 words, specific, no exclamation marks]

---

[OPENING SENTENCE — delivers value immediately; no preamble]

[PARAGRAPH 2 — context or the surprising detail]

[PARAGRAPH 3 — the insight or implication that makes this worth knowing]

[OPTIONAL PARAGRAPH 4 — a specific example or stat that grounds the abstract]

[CLOSE — 1 sentence setting up the link]

[LINK PROMPT: "Read the full post →" or "Full breakdown here →"]
```

### Minimum atom requirements
- At least 1 stat OR insight atom in the body
- Total atoms reflected: at least 2

### Tone register
- Editorial, warm, authoritative. "A colleague who reads a lot."
- Second person ("you") acceptable but not required.
- The reader is smart — don't over-explain. Surface what's surprising, skip what's obvious.
- Respect their time: if the first sentence doesn't earn attention, they won't read the second.

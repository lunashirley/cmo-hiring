## Video Script Template

### Target duration
60 seconds (130–150 words spoken at ~2.5 words/second)

### Required sections
| Section | Timing | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Auditory Hook | 0–3s | Pattern interrupt — stops the scroll |
| Value Gap / Bridge | 3–15s | Stakes + curiosity gap to be resolved |
| Payoff / Delivery | 15–50s | 1-2-3 structured delivery of the core value |
| Loop CTA | 50–60s | Incomplete thought or segue back to hook + low-friction CTA |

### Script format per line
```
[Visual Action]: Description of what appears on screen
"Dialogue": Exact spoken words
```

### Structural constraints
- Word count: 130–150 words total (spoken dialogue only; visual cues are not counted)
- Hook: first sentence must be a Pattern Interrupt — Negative Hook, Result-First, or High-Stakes Question
- No introductions ("Hey guys", "Welcome back", "In today's video")
- No filler words in dialogue ("um", "uh", "so", "basically", "you know")
- Payoff section must use a numbered structure (1, 2, 3) with [Visual Transition] or [Text Overlay] cues between points
- Loop CTA: final sentence leads back into the hook OR delivers a low-friction micro-CTA
- Short, punchy sentences throughout — maximum 12 words per sentence in the hook and bridge

### Tone register
- Conversational, high-energy, rhythmic
- Sounds natural when read aloud
- Active voice, present tense preferred
- No corporate-speak; reads like a person talking, not a brand announcement

### Capacity Planning: Stop Guessing, Start Thriving

You're busy managing demand, but what if you could predict your peak periods with accuracy? Many service businesses face the challenge of either underestimating their capacity or being overwhelmed during busy seasons.

The data reveals a clear pattern: 40% of appointments are booked outside business hours, meaning your doors aren't closing on potential revenue. Meanwhile, online booked appointments boast a no-show rate of just 1.8%, compared to 5.9% for phone bookings.

Understanding your real capacity is the first step to optimizing your business. Analyze your scheduling calendar to determine your actual limits before demand surges. Then, leverage automated reminders via SMS or email to reduce no-shows by up to 38%, maximizing your revenue.

This approach frees up your time—automate admin tasks like booking confirmations and reminders so you can focus on core business activities during high-volume periods.

Navigating male-dominated spaces isn't always easy. But what happens when passion meets persistence?

Ovsanna Harutyunyan, founder of Brilliant Cut, didn't set out to revolutionize barbershops—she simply tried styling hair one day and discovered her calling.

'There's a stereotype. Some don't take us seriously. But women barbers are common elsewhere,' she reflects.

Her journey? Pure coincidence turned purpose. 'I never intended to be a hairdresser or barber. It was a fluke, and I got hooked.'

This isn't just a story about breaking barriers—it's about authenticity. When you do what you love, client loyalty follows. And sometimes, the unexpected path leads to your true calling.

Curious how passion can turn into a thriving business?

Meet Ovsanna Harutyunyan, founder of Brilliant Cut in Brno. Originally from Armenia, Ovsanna didn't start out dreaming of becoming a barber—but when she tried it, the rest followed.

### A story of dedication

Ovsanna never intended to become a hairdresser. It started as a simple experiment, something she thought she'd try once. But it captured her attention so completely that she dedicated herself fully to it.

### More than just a job

For Ovsanna, it's about passion and doing things right. "We are women in a traditionally male barbershop setting, and our quality comes from doing it right with dedication and heart," she adds.

### What's your biggest scheduling headache?

Ovsanna's clients are incredibly loyal—often finding her after leaving previous jobs. It speaks volumes about how a genuine passion and quality service can build lasting relationships.

Cash flow stress plagues many service businesses, but you can manage it. Here’s how to clean up your operations without overhauling your systems.

The problem often starts with outdated client data or late payments. A clean client database removes duplicate contacts, while chasing overdue invoices prevents cash from sitting idle.

Action: Remove duplicate contacts and outdated contacts from your client database. Then, review your pricing and chase overdue invoices. Reconnect with lapsed clients before your calendar fills up — they represent untapped revenue.

Late payments are a consistent source of cash flow stress. By managing invoices proactively, you can avoid this drain.

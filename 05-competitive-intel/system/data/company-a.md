# Company A: Smartsupp

## ICP Definition
Mid-market e-commerce retailers (€5M–€20M revenue) in Europe who need to scale customer support and conversion without proportional headcount growth. Three primary personas: (1) growth-stage e-shops outgrowing manual support, (2) complex-product retailers (furniture, auto, high-tech) where visual context accelerates resolution, and (3) omnichannel brands managing high volumes across WhatsApp, Facebook Messenger, and live chat from a single inbox. Buying triggers: rising support ticket volume, cart abandonment rate concerns, and pressure to improve conversion without hiring more agents.

## Our Positioning
Smartsupp is the Autonomous Revenue Engine for e-commerce — not a chat widget, but an AI Shopping Assistant that converts. The core narrative: the combination of **Mira AI** (autonomous resolution, handles up to 96% of inquiries) and **Visitor Video Recordings** (behavioral context no competitor offers) creates a feedback loop that maximizes e-commerce ROI. We don't just deflect tickets; we generate leads and grow revenue.

Headline: *"Turn conversations into conversions."*

## Key Differentiators
1. **Visitor Video Recordings** — unique in the market; gives agents and AI visual behavioral context (rage clicks, navigation paths) that no pure-chat competitor can match. Critical for complex-product retailers.
2. **Mira AI autonomy** — proven 96% inquiry automation (Leader Fox case study). Not just an assist layer; a fully autonomous agent with product feed integration (30k items), real-time inventory awareness, and markdown-formatted responses.
3. **Price-to-power ratio** — Video Recordings + omnichannel (WhatsApp, FB Messenger, Email) + AI starts at $17/mo. Enterprise-tier features at SMB-tier prices.
4. **Proactive engagement** — automated messages, visitor list engagement, and multi-language flows let teams reach visitors before they ask, not just respond after.
5. **Proven e-commerce ROI** — case studies show concrete revenue impact (Breno: +169% conversion, +10.8% revenue; AAA AUTO: 7,200 leads/mo).

## Known Weaknesses / Objections
- **Mira AI is an add-on, not included** — prospects comparing all-in pricing will see a higher effective cost than headline plan prices suggest; objection: "the AI costs extra on top."
- **Operator seat limits on lower plans** — Solo (1 operator) and Plus (3 operators) cap growth; teams that scale quickly hit a wall before Expert.
- **Single website on Solo/Plus** — multi-site brands must jump to Expert ($83/mo) for 3 websites; no middle tier.
- **Limited admin controls below Expert** — agent groups, automated tagging, visitor filters, and team shortcuts are Expert-only; can feel restrictive for Plus-tier teams.
- **Free plan is very limited** — 25 conversations/month, 14-day history, no AI: useful for demos but not real trialing.
- **API customization** — available as an add-on at Expert, included only at Ultimate; technically demanding customers may feel gated.
